Scallop Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (linguine or fettuccine)
  • 10-12 sea scallops
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup white wine (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Pat scallops dry, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear scallops for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  4. Reduce heat to medium, add butter and garlic, and saut√© for 1 minute.
  5. Add white wine (if using) and let it reduce for a minute.
  6. Pour in heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, stirring until smooth.
  7. Toss cooked pasta into the sauce and mix well.
  8. Return scallops to the pan and gently combine.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot. Enjoy!
