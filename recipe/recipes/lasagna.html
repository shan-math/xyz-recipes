Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Vegetable or meat mixture
  • Cherry tomato and basil sauce
  • Pesto sauce
  • Cheese mixture

Instructions

  1. Grease a baking dish with butter and spread a layer of tomato sauce.
  2. Place cooked al dente lasagna sheets on top.
  3. Add a layer of the vegetable/meat mixture, followed by pesto sauce and cheese.
  4. Repeat the layering process as needed.
  5. Bake at 340°F (170°C) for 10 minutes. Enjoy!
