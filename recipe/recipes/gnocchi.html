Gnocchi

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter and sage

Instructions

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Mix in flour, eggs, and cheese to form a dough.
  3. Roll and cut into small pieces.
  4. Boil until gnocchi floats.
  5. Saut√© with butter and sage. Enjoy!
